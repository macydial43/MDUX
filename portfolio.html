"Research is formalized curiosity. It is poking and prying with a purpose."

- Zora Neale Hurston

My name is Macy Dial, and growing up in the heart of Provo, Utah, has shaped my passion for research. I remember having so many questions about the unique cultural landscape around me—more specifically, I was puzzled by the power dynamics, structural inequality and social norms I witnessed. When I discovered sociology in college, it validated not only that my questions were relevant, but it offered a framework to answering those questions. I have since learned that quality research can offer critical context to understand the world around me and how it’s changing.

Since discovering the world of UX and design thinking, I’ve realized that I can use my curiosity and research skills to better understand how to design and create solutions for other people. This discovery has led me to love product management, where research, design, and business strategy come together to develop tools that can change the world for the better.

I'm passionate about UX, research, people, and inclusivity.
